01 · The Problem

Sales training is fundamentally broken.

Companies spend billions on enablement that never translates to performance. We identified the four root causes — and built Qirvex to solve each one.

$20B+
The Cost of the Status Quo

Spent annually on sales training with minimal measurable ROI. 40–50% of reps still miss quota. The problem isn't pipeline — it's a skills gap that no LMS has ever solved.

01 · Vacuum

Training happens in isolation

Reps complete LMS courses disconnected from real deals. They watch videos about discovery best practices but never apply them under live conditions. By the time they need it, 80% is gone.

02 · Measurement

We measure the wrong things

"90% training completion rate" tells you nothing about capability. We track activities — calls made, emails sent — not quality. Managers discover skill gaps when deals are already lost.

03 · Knowledge

Best practices die with top performers

Your top 10% have figured out what wins. That knowledge stays locked in their heads. There's no systematic way to extract those patterns and scale them across the rest of the team.

04 · Coaching

Coaching doesn't scale

Great managers provide contextual coaching, but there aren't enough hours. Feedback comes days after a call — too late to matter. Junior reps who need it most get it least.
